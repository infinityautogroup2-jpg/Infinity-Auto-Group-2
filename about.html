About Infinity Auto Group

Culver City, CA team focused on practical, affordable driving — rentals, mobile mechanical help, and shop services.

Our Story

Five years ago, the desire to help people with trustworthy, budget‑friendly, and reliable automotive services came to fruition with Infinity Auto Group. Since then, hundreds of clients have been satisfied with the services we provide.

We know the struggles because we were once in the same place as you — needing a car for a few weeks because ours broke down and not being able to afford the insane prices that big companies charge. Or searching for a car that will last, without hidden problems or quick fixes. Or being stranded far from home and wishing a mechanic could come to us.

Infinity Auto Group was created to make these wishes come true. Whether you need an affordable rental, a dependable car to buy, or a mechanic who comes to you, our goal is to be that helping hand.
